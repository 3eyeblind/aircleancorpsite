Why Clean Your HVAC System?

Dirty HVAC systems cost money and compromise health. Regular professional cleaning delivers measurable benefits.

Improve Air Quality

Remove accumulated dust, debris, mold, and allergens from your air handling systems so you create a healthier environment for occupants.

Increase Efficiency

Clean systems run more efficiently. Reduce energy consumption and lower utility costs by eliminating buildup that forces your equipment to work harder.

Extend Equipment Life

Preventative maintenance significantly extends the lifespan of your HVAC equipment because it protects your capital investment and helps you avoid costly replacements.

The Hidden Dangers of Neglected HVAC Systems

Dirty ducts and uncleaned HVAC systems are more than just inefficient. They can become a serious health and safety risk. Buildup of contaminants recirculates through your facility whenever the system runs, which means occupants continue breathing contaminated air.

  • Mold and mildew growth in ductwork
  • Reduced airflow straining equipment
  • Allergens and pollutants circulated to occupants
  • Increased risk of equipment failure
  • Higher energy costs and carbon footprint
  • Potential fire hazards in dryer and range exhaust systems

Don't wait until there's a problem.

Preventative maintenance is always less expensive than emergency repairs or equipment replacement because it identifies problems early and keeps systems running efficiently. Air Clean Corp has been protecting Virginia facilities since 1978 so you can trust us to protect yours as well.
